FAQ

Common Questions

How do I get an estimate?

Simply call us at (587) 894-9480 or fill out the form above. We'll schedule a free on-site visit at a time that works for you, assess the project, and provide a detailed written quote — usually within 24 hours of the visit.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Luso Painting Inc is fully licensed as a painting contractor in Alberta and carries comprehensive general liability insurance. We're happy to provide proof of insurance upon request.

What areas do you serve?

We serve all of Calgary (NW, NE, SW, SE, and downtown) as well as Airdrie, Cochrane, Chestermere, Okotoks, Strathmore, and High River. Not sure if we cover your area? Just call us.

How long does a typical interior painting project take?

It depends on the size and complexity of the project. A standard room takes 1 day; a full interior of an average home typically takes 3–5 days. We'll give you a clear timeline in your quote.

Do I need to supply the paint or materials?

No, we supply everything. We use premium paints from trusted brands like Benjamin Moore and Sherwin-Williams. You'll have the opportunity to choose your colours — we'll guide you through the options.

Do you offer a warranty on your work?

Yes. We stand behind our craftsmanship. If any issues arise due to our workmanship within a reasonable period after project completion, we'll come back and make it right — at no cost to you.
