Our Story

Built on Trust & Quality

Luso Painting Inc was founded with a simple belief: every space deserves a beautiful, lasting finish. What started as a small local operation has grown into one of Calgary's most trusted painting contractors.

We bring Portuguese craftsmanship traditions — attention to detail, pride in workmanship, and a genuine care for the client — to every project we take on. From single-room refreshes to full commercial repaint jobs, we approach each project with the same dedication.

Our team is fully licensed and insured, and we use only premium-quality paints and materials that stand up to Calgary's extreme climate swings, ensuring your investment lasts for years.
